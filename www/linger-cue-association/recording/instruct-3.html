START SCREEN RECORDING

This screen recording can be captured using the Zoom application. If you do not have Zoom downloaded, you may download it using this site: Download Zoom (link opens in new tab).

Once you have downloaded Zoom, follow these steps to screen record:

  1. Close all windows on your computer except your web browser with this experiment.
  2. Open Zoom and start a new meeting.
  3. Change your name.
  4. Click the Share Screen button.
  5. Choose the browser window you want to share (do not share your entire screen).
  6. Click Record in the Zoom toolbar to start the recording.
  7. When you finish, click Stop Recording in the Zoom toolbar.
  8. The recording will be saved to your computer automatically.

If you would like to record your screen using another application, you are more than welcome to do so. Please, just ensure that it is a recording of only your browser, does not expose your personally identifying information, and you can export the recording as an .mp4 file.
