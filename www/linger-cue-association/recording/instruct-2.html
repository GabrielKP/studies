Screen Recording

The recording will help us verify that you are attentive and do not use AI during the experiment.

The recording will not extend beyond the boundaries of the web browser (e.g., it will not include your computer desktop or other applications), and it will be limited to the duration of the experiment only. Any such screen recording will not be used for advertising or non-study related purposes.

You should know that

  • We will only use these screen recordings for the purposes of this research. We will not make them publicly available.
  • If you agree to screen recording and then change your mind, you may ask us to destroy the recording for any reason.

Before you start the recording, close all windows other than your current web browser. You do not need to use an incognito window, but make sure to close all browser tabs that are open right now, except the one for the experiment.

Be careful not to accidentally close the browser tab/window for the experiment, or your data may be lost.
