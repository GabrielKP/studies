Purple sentences

In this study, you will be asked to read different stories.

The color of the story text and the pace at which they are presented will vary, but your task will always be: to attend to and understand each narrative.

Most of the time, the story will be shown in black text with a white background.

Sometimes you will be asked to read a different story, shown in purple.

For the purple sentences, each sentence will be displayed for a fixed amount of time, after which the next sentence will be shown.
